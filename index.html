Vedanta Foundation equip Nand Ghars with smart TVs and solar panels for reliable power so that children can complete their curriculum through e-learning, BaLA designs and smart kits. More than 2000 Nand Ghars are running across Rajasthan, Odisha, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh. And Vedanta Foundation aims to construct 2000 more across India. Your steps will enable the infrastructure for e-learning of thousands of children.


From the past 28 years, over 170 centres nationwide have turned around the lives of about 1.4 million beneficiaries through its flagship vertical- “Vocational Training Program.”
In 2020, the vertical was revamped and the vocational training centers were metamorphosed into an Employment Linked Skill Training Program called the Vedanta Rojgar Yojana. Within a year, the Rojgar Yojana has upskilled around 8500 unskilled youths, of whom, around 2000 beneficiaries were instantly placed during the placement drives held by the Vedanta Foundation and its stakeholders.

Vision & Mission

Vision

The Foundation envisions to uplift the underprivileged communities through education and skill development and aims at providing livelihood opportunities to them. After transforming millions of lives through various ambitious initiatives, Vedanta Foundation aspires to widen its scope and function to reach out to the population of billions. The Foundation envisions a world where nutrition, education, and livelihood are not a deprivation. For this, the Foundation has established placement cells at its skilling centers, provides job and internship opportunities to its beneficiaries, and constantly monitors their growth.

Mission

Vedanta Foundation aims at making its skilling centers top notch in the country by 2022 and laying out the best college module by 2023. The Foundation is set out to create the largest hub for employment and education in the country.

In 1992, Vedanta Foundation embarked on a journey to create a Better World; with resolute and firm determination. Heretofore the foundation works for the betterment of less privileged communities.
